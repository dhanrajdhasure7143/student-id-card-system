1. Logging In

  • Click on the "Login" button at the top right of the page.
  • Enter your email and password.
  • Click "Login" to access the admin panel.

2. Navigating the Admin Panel

  • Use "Add Student" to register a new student.
  • Use "Student List" to view/edit/delete students.
  • Click "Logout" when you're done.

3. Adding a New Student

  • Click "Add Student".
  • Enter name, info, and photo.
  • Click "Save".

4. Editing an Existing Student

  • Click the row of the student you wish to edit.
  • Edit name/info/photo directly in the table.
  • Click "Update" to save changes.

5. Deleting a Student

  • Select the student's row.
  • Click the "Delete" button.

6. Viewing a Student's ID Card

  • Select the student.
  • Click "View ID Card".

7. Updating a Student's Details

  • Edit the student's info as needed.
  • Click "Update" to save your changes.
Tips & Reminders:
  • Always click "Update" to save your edits.
  • Use "View ID Card" to preview student IDs.
  • Use "Delete" to remove a student permanently.
  • Logout when your work is complete.
